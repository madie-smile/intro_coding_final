HUNGRY WORM

Your worm is hungry for circles!

Use your MOUSE to eat as many circles as you can in 30 seconds. Hold down the RIGHT ARROW to grow.

Watch out for bombs! Hold down the LEFT ARROW to shrink past falling bombs.
